About

This game has no name. It is a game with perma-death meaning if you die, you will need to refresh your browser to start over. The game has 3 waves followed by a final boss. You can spend stat points on upgrades for your character, these upgrades include increases to health, damage and speed.

Controls

Use the controls WASD to move your character around the screen. Use the keys jkli to shoot in 4 corresponding directions. Refresh the page to start over and click the green buttons to the right of the stats to spend your skill points.

How it works

In this game you level up by defeating enemies. Once you level up you get skill points you can spend on the top of the gameplay screen. On top of the gameplay screen you'll notice a gui including level, skills, sp (skill points) and a green dot representing your health. When you spend skill points on your health you'll gain more green dots until you take damage. Enemies have a chance of dropping green circular health ups. These will repair a health point each, only use them when you lose health though otherwise it may be wasted.


Once your ready to play click the PLAY button on the top right of this page.

Cheats

The number keys 4-7 (and 0) can be used to add cheats to your game. The cheats give you benefits as follows:

4:
Increase the current wave. After hitting wave 3 this key will spawn bosses.

5:
Increase xp by 500. (1 press will make your character instantly level 7)

6:
Increase health points by 99999. (Will not display 99999 green hp dots)

7:
Increase damage by 999. (One-shot everything)

0:
Break the bounds of the world!!!
